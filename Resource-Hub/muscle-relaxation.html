The Process

Progressive Muscle Relaxation (PMR) is a two-step process. First, you **tense** a group of muscles. Then, you **release** the tension. This helps you become aware of how muscle tension feels and learn to intentionally relax your body.

How to do it:

  • **Find a comfortable position.** Lie down or sit in a chair with your feet flat on the floor and your hands on your lap.
  • **Start at your head and work your way down.** The guided audio will instruct you to tense specific muscle groups, such as your forehead, jaw, neck, and shoulders.
  • **Tense each muscle group for 5 seconds.** Squeeze the muscles tightly, but not so hard that it causes pain.
  • **Release the tension.** For 15 seconds, focus on the sensation of relaxation and how different it feels from being tense.
  • **Continue to move through all major muscle groups** until you feel a deep sense of calm and relaxation throughout your body.

Benefits of PMR

  • Reduces anxiety and stress
  • Helps you fall asleep faster
  • Relieves chronic pain and muscle tension
  • Lowers blood pressure
  • Increases body awareness

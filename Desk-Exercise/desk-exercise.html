Desk Stretches

A guided 10-minute routine to relieve neck, shoulder, and back tension.

10:00
Get ready!

Follow these steps:

  • 1

    Neck Rolls

    Slowly roll your head from side to side.

  • 2

    Shoulder Shrugs

    Lift your shoulders up to your ears, then relax.

  • 3

    Cat-Cow Stretch

    Arch and round your back in your chair.

  • 4

    Spinal Twist

    Gently twist your torso to one side, then the other.

  • 5

    Wrist Stretches

    Extend your arm and gently pull your hand back.

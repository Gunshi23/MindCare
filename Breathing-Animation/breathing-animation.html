4-7-8 Breathing Technique

A simple yet powerful relaxation technique to calm your nervous system. "The natural tranquilizer for the nervous system."

How to Perform the 4-7-8 Breathing:

  • timer_10 **Inhale (4 seconds):** Breathe in quietly through your nose for 4 counts.
  • pause **Hold (7 seconds):** Hold your breath for 7 counts.
  • air **Exhale (8 seconds):** Exhale completely through your mouth, making a whoosh sound, for 8 counts.
  • loop Repeat for 3-4 cycles.
